Meet Iber Cutlery

Where precision meets Portuguese craftsmanship.

Who We Are

Iber Cutlery is a factory located in Braga, Portugal, specializing in the production of premium stainless steel cutlery. Our commitment to excellence combines cutting-edge technology with handcrafted precision, delivering products that reflect the true spirit of Portuguese industry.

Our People

Behind every piece of cutlery stands a team of professionals passionate about design, engineering, and quality. Iber Cutlery’s success is built on decades of experience, innovation, and attention to detail.
